# Buy Me a Coffee About Text

Hi, I'm Anatoli, creator of ANEVRED.

ANEVRED (Adaptive Neural Enhanced Visual Recognition Engine Daemon) is a Windows utility focused on gaming optimization, AI-assisted overlays, OCR-based live translation, Star Citizen tools, adaptive screen dimming, hardware monitoring, and quality-of-life features for PC gamers.

The project started as a personal tool to avoid unnecessary alt-tabbing during gameplay and has grown into a broader gaming assistance platform.

If ANEVRED helped you improve visibility, translate missions, monitor performance, or simply enjoy your gaming sessions more, consider supporting future development.

Every coffee helps fund new features, testing, and continued updates.

Thank you for being part of the journey.
